Hi, I'm Schalk.

I call the Bushveld my home.

You'll find me in a small town called Modimolle, better known as Nylstroom, in the southern part of Limpopo. It is about a 120km drive from Tshwane (Pretoria) in Gauteng. The Waterberg district is arguably the hunting mecca of South Africa. It's a hide-away to soak in the beauty of nature and recharge your life force. I love it here!

When I'm not engaged in web development (or staring at my computer monitor) I enjoy spending time with my family, especially outdoors. Hiking is fun! When cabin fever sets in there's enough blue skyes to lift the winter blues. I love to keep fit and there's no better way to do than doing it close to nature together with your family. It is also a great chance to learn more and see more.

I love braaivleis (meat grilled over an open fire), biltong (strips of meat cured by adding salt and spices, and then drying), boerewors (traditional Afrikaner sausage) and potjiekos (traditional Afrikaner dish prepared outside in a pot over an open fire). I also love to read and play guitar. Lately I've been neglecting my guitar, but every now and again I pick up my guitar and strum my worries away....

I fell in love with web development in 2020 and I'm constantly improving my programming skills to deliver top notch web development services. I've switched to web/software development fairly late in life. And although the learning curve is quite steep, especially with the fast pace at which all the technologies evovle, I enjoy all it has to offer. I guess it is the whole process of starting a project from scratch; developing it; improving it; until the finished product comes to life! Creation is truly liberating!
